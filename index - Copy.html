My journey to the 'QUEEN' of hill stations; Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udagamandalam is a beautiful hill station located in the nilgiri district of tamil nadu.It is situates at an altitude of 2,240 meters above sea level and is known

for its cool climate, stunning views and picturesque surroundings. Ooty is a popular vacation spot for tourists from all over the india and is known for its breathtaking views,

Colonial-style archetechture and delcious cusines.

As I started my journey to this charming hill station , I found myself captivated by the allurye of its verdent hills, coloanial archetechture,

and the fragrant aroma of eucalyptus that lingered in the crisp mountain air.

The Nilgiri Mounatian Railway

My adventure began by aboarding the nilgiri mountain railway, a UNESCO world heritge site.The vintage blue train chugged its way through tnnels and over viaducts,

offering breathtaking panoramas of tea plantaions and mist kissed valleys.It was nostalgic ride that transported me back to a bygone era

I relly enjoyed the ride and there was a rush of strong emotions while riding the train.I was really overwhelmed by this train ride. It was

very beautiful.

Ooty lake

Then i went to a boat ride on Ooty lake. T he lake offered a tranquil escape. Surrounded by eucalyptus groves, the lake mirrored the clear blue

skies and emerald hills. As the oars gently sliced through the water, I embracec the serenity that enveloped me.

Of all the places i went to Ooty, this lake was my personal favorite one.

Ooty Rose Garden

The Ooty rose gardens, a symphony of colours and fragrances, showcased thousands of rose varieties. The manicured lawn and blooming roses

provised a picturesque setting , inviting visitors to immerse themselves in nature's beauty. The flowers are arranged in five curvng terraces

covering four hectares. The garden is maintained by the Tamil Nadu Horiculture Department.

During the time i was in Ooty i enjoyed most of there local cuisines like;

  • Nilgiri Tea
  • Ooty varkey: a crunchy, baked sweet biscuit
  • Homemade chocolates
  • Keema unde: Flavourful meatball , crafted with local spices
  • Ooty varkey icecream: combines dish of Ooty varkey and creamy icecream
